About Kitchen Door

Kitchen Door began with a simple idea: that the best food memories often happen just beyond the doorway. A warm kitchen. A shared table. A sweet treat passed around without ceremony.

Our café brings together flavours from different cultures and serves them in a light-hearted, playful way. Familiar comforts sit alongside small discoveries — simple bakes, classic sweets, and recipes shaped by many places.

Food doesn’t need explaining. It just needs to be shared.

Inspired by London

London is a city of many kitchens. Living here means meeting people from everywhere — and often, tasting their food before you know their stories.

Kitchen Door is shaped by that quiet exchange: recipes passed between cultures, flavours borrowed and re-imagined, and the feeling that you don’t have to travel far to taste somewhere else.

Behind the Door

Kitchen Door is created by one pair of hands, guided by curiosity and a love for simple treats. Some stories are written on menus. Others are left for you to discover.
