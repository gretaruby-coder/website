ABOUT US

Food Express is a quaint cafe located in the heart of Panjim city. It was started in 2010 by Greta Fernandes. She shared a passion for cooking and baking and wanted to open her own place and thus Food Express was born.
Food Express started off as a small cafe serving only coffee and snacks and as time passed by, it became more popular. Now after only 5 years, Food Express has become one of the top cafes in Goa with a wider menu and caters to different people.
At Food Express, we use only thefinest and freshest ingredients.

The store is managed and run by Greta, who also works behind-the-scenes in the kitchen along with Chef Tanvi to prepare the food and drinks, that all of our customers have come to love.
So come visit us and have a bite, for the love of food!
