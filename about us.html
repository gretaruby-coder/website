About Kitchen Door

Kitchen Door is a quaint café tucked away in the heart of London. Founded in 2010 by Greta Fernandes, the café was born from her lifelong passion for cooking and baking, and her dream of creating a welcoming space where people could enjoy thoughtfully prepared food.

At Kitchen Door, we believe that great flavour starts with great ingredients. That’s why we use only the finest and freshest produce in everything we make. The café is run by Greta herself, who works both front-of-house and behind the scenes in the kitchen, lovingly preparing the food and drinks that our customers have come to know and enjoy.

So come visit us and have a bite — for the love of food.
